# Fundamental Rights (UPSC Polity)

Fundamental Rights are enshrined in Part III of the Constitution of India.
They include:
- Right to Equality
- Right to Freedom
- Right against Exploitation
- Right to Freedom of Religion
- Cultural and Educational Rights
- Right to Constitutional Remedies

Articles 12 to 35 broadly cover Fundamental Rights.
Writs under Article 32 and Article 226 are key enforcement mechanisms.
